Our Story

Crafting Elegance,
One Stitch at a Time

Seema Silai Centre was born from a lifelong passion for the art of tailoring. Founded by Seema Ji in 2008, our atelier has grown from a humble neighbourhood studio into a beloved destination for women seeking garments that truly fit—body, soul, and style.

Every garment that leaves our studio carries with it hours of careful measurement, hand-sewn precision, and an intimate understanding of how fabric drapes, flows, and flatters. We believe clothing is not merely functional—it is an expression of identity.

From intricate bridal lehengas adorned with delicate hand embroidery, to impeccably fitted everyday salwar suits, we treat each project with equal reverence and skill.

Seema Devi Master Tailor & Founder
